Study Content

This study conducts a basic investigation of the characteristics of gentle stroking tactile stimulation on the arm, and examines the pain-reducing effects of such tactile sensations.

Pain reduction research through gentle tactile stimulation

The experiment involves applying gentle tactile stimulation to the arm, while simultaneously presenting thermal stimulation equivalent to Pain-60 (pain that makes it difficult to concentrate on something) as a pain stimulus.
