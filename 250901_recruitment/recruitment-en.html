Study Content

This study conducts a basic investigation of the characteristics of gentle stroking tactile stimulation on the arm, and examines the pain-reducing effects of such tactile sensations.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notice

The experiment involves applying gentle tactile stimulation to the arm, while simultaneously presenting thermal stimulation equivalent to Pain-60 (pain that makes it difficult to concentrate on something) as a pain stimulus.

  • Temporary pain will be involved
  • Slight discomfort may be experienced
  • Please participate only if you agree after understanding the safety considerations below

üõ°Ô∏è Safety Considerations

The thermal stimulation presented in this experiment is delivered through a protocol with established safety used in existing pain research, including prior studies.

Participant Rights:

  • Right to immediately stop the thermal stimulation being presented
  • Right to pause or discontinue the experiment itself

Safety Devices:

  • Safety device to reset stimulation site to room temperature
  • Safety device allowing participants to immediately stop thermal stimulation

Insurance Coverage: In the unlikely event of injury or harm from this experiment, compensation will be provided through the National University Association Insurance.
